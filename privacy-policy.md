# Privacy Policy for TimeKind

**Last Updated**: March 1, 2026  
**Effective Date**: March 1, 2026

## 1. Overview

TimeKind ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application.

**Core Principle**: TimeKind is designed as an offline-first app. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data about your tasks, routines, or activities by default.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

TimeKind does **not** collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Task data or content (what tasks you create)
- Location data or GPS information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Browsing history or usage patterns
- Biometric data
- Payment information

### 2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

All data created in TimeKind is stored locally on your device only:
- Tasks and task details
- Time tracking records
- Journal entries and notes
- User preferences and settings
- Language and theme selections

**You have complete control** over this data and can delete it anytime through the app's Settings menu.

### 2.3 Optional Analytics (If You Enable It)

If you **opt-in** to analytics in Settings, we collect:
- App feature usage patterns (anonymous)
- Which screens you visit
- How long you spend in the app
- Which features you use most
- Device OS version
- App version number

**This data is anonymous** and does not identify you personally. It helps us understand which features are most valuable and where to focus improvements.

### 2.4 Optional Crash Reporting (If You Enable It)

If you **opt-in** to crash reporting in Settings, we collect:
- Crash stack traces (technical error information)
- Device OS version
- App version number
- Timestamp of the crash

**This data is anonymous** and does not include any of your task data or personal information. It helps us identify and fix bugs.

### 2.5 Permissions

TimeKind requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required | Optional |
|-----------|---------|----------|----------|
| Microphone | Record audio notes for tasks | No | Yes |
| Notifications | Send task reminders and alerts | No | Yes |
| Storage | Export and backup your data | No | Yes |

You can grant or deny these permissions at any time through your device's Settings.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

- **Local Storage**: All task data is stored on your device using encrypted local storage
- **No Cloud Backup**: We do not automatically back up your data to cloud servers
- **No Sync**: Your data does not sync across devices
- **Your Control**: You can export or delete your data anytime

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:
- Data is encrypted at rest on your device
- App uses HTTPS for any network communication
- No sensitive data is transmitted without encryption
- Regular security updates through app updates

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained on your device until you delete it
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 90 days
- **Crash Reports**: Retained for up to 30 days
- **You can delete all data**: Settings → Clear All Data

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access Your Data

You can access all your data anytime:
- Open TimeKind app
- Go to Settings
- View all your tasks, entries, and preferences

### 6.2 Export Your Data

You can export your data anytime:
- Open TimeKind app
- Go to Settings → Export Data
- Download your data as JSON or CSV

### 6.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data anytime:
- Open TimeKind app
- Go to Settings → Clear All Data
- Confirm deletion

**Note**: This permanently deletes all local data. This action cannot be undone.

### 6.4 Disable Analytics & Crash Reporting

You can disable optional data collection:
- Open TimeKind app
- Go to Settings → Privacy
- Toggle Analytics OFF
- Toggle Crash Reporting OFF

### 6.5 Right to Be Forgotten

You have the right to request deletion of all your data:
- Contact us at: nemgmedia@gmail.com
- We will delete all data associated with your request within 30 days

## 16. FAQ

**Q: Does TimeKind sell my data?**  
A: No. We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

**Q: Is my data backed up to the cloud?**  
A: No. Your data stays on your device only. We do not provide automatic cloud backup.

**Q: Can I access my data on another device?**  
A: No. Data is stored locally on each device. To use your data on another device, you must export it from one device and import it on another.

**Q: What happens if I uninstall the app?**  
A: Your local data will be deleted from your device. We recommend exporting your data before uninstalling.

**Q: How do I know what analytics data is collected?**  
A: You can review the analytics in Settings → Privacy. We are transparent about what we collect.

**Q: Can I use TimeKind without enabling analytics?**  
A: Yes. Analytics is optional and disabled by default. The app works fully without it.

**Q: How long does Google keep my crash reports?**  
A: Google retains crash reports for 30 days. After that, they are automatically deleted.

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**  
A: Yes. Your local data is encrypted at rest on your device. Any network communication uses HTTPS encryption.
